About Me

I aspire to become a professional Financial Analyst within the next three years, driven by a passion for storytelling with numbers. For me, financial analysis is not just about crunching data but about uncovering what those numbers mean for a company’s growth, stability, or challenges—and translating them into actionable insights.
Curiosity, openness to feedback, and a willingness to grow guide my approach. I am continuously strengthening my technical toolkit—Excel, SQL, Power BI, Tableau, and Python—allowing me to choose the right tool for building reports, models, forecasts, and data visualizations. My goal is always to uncover the “why” behind the numbers and support strategic decision-making.
With the rapid development of AI, I am also expanding my skills in applying AI tools to enhance efficiency and the quality of my work. Beyond technical expertise, I value empathy in professional settings—balancing facts with an understanding of diverse perspectives, emotions, and uncertainties. This enables me to build stronger connections with both teammates and clients.
Recalling my time at Philips, I developed a Funnel Power BI dashboard that improved project tracking and data consistency by 30% and increased the accuracy of actual-versus-plan tracking by 80% through a financial model. This project directly contributed to identifying improvement opportunities. My experience working with stakeholders across international markets has strengthened my ability to collaborate effectively and adapt quickly to new business environments.
Outside of work, I invest in my physical and mental well-being through yoga, staying informed by reading financial and global news, and broadening my perspective by learning Dutch.
